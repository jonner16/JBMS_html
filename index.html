    About Us

    We offer a beautiful ceremony without temple membership. A JBMS Ceremony is rich in Jewish wisdom, inspiring music, and lasting feelings of warmth and pride. The JBMS service includes a beautiful Bima, creating a synagogue setting in the location of your choice. Contact us and hear how easily JBMS can make your Bar/Bat Mitzvah experience the very finest.

    What We Provide

    Study Program

    We provide assistance in finding a suitable mentor to prepare our students for their Torah reading. No previous Hebrew or religious school is necessary. Students learn all the Hebrew prayers, Torah chanting, and English readings for their service. Students are guided towards important ideas and Jewish values found in the Torah portion. Students learn to project the Hebrew prayers and English readings at their service with confidence.

    Bima Lending

    JBMS offers you a complete and elegant Bima, including an authentic handwritten Torah with silver Yad, a Torah Ark, a specially designed Torah table, matching Cantor’s podium, decorative side panels, and an excellent sound system, converting your location into a lovely synagogue setting. The JBMS Bima is perfect for both inside and outside venues. The Bima is set up prior to the ceremony, allowing plenty of time to capture precious family photos with your beautiful Bima as a matching background. The Bima is also available as a rental even if JBMS does not conduct your service. For more detailed information regarding Bima rental, please contact us

    Our Story

    JBMS, the Jewish Bar & Bat Mitzvah Service, was created in 1994 by Jewish educator Owen ‘Mar’ Meldy. ‘Mar’ means ‘Mr’ in Hebrew and is also related to the word for ‘teacher.’ Raised in Los Angeles, Owen lived and worked in Israel, where he graduated the Hebrew University, Jerusalem. After specializing in Jewish Education in Los Angeles, Mar Meldy was put in charge of Bar/Bat Mitzvah training at Stephen S. Wise Temple where he was promoted to Program Director for the entire temple. Mar Meldy created and taught a highly successful spoken Hebrew language program at the University Of Judaism. Realizing that approximately 80% of all Jews in Los Angeles were not affiliated with any organized Jewish institution, temple or synagogue, Owen created JBMS, an independent Jewish educational program devoted to Bar/Bat Mitzvah for every Jew. JBMS serves Los Angeles and the greater Southern California area. The one-time fee is very reasonable, allowing every Jewish family to exercise its birthright of Bar/Bat Mitzvah and fulfill the mitzvah for their children. Since a religiously binding Bar/Bat Mitzvah service requires a Minyan of 10 adults, JBMS is pleased to provide you and your family with a beautiful and meaningful ceremony conducted in the location of your choice. 'Mar' Meldy's teaching approach conveys the beauty and personal significance of Judaism to the individual. Jewish concepts are presented as life relevant humanistic values that foster the personal development of each Bar or Bat mitzvah's life adventure into adulthood. You are invited to read testimonial letters written by families who have experienced the JBMS Study Program and Religious Service. Please contact us with any questions you may have in making your Bar/Bat Mitzvah ceremony all that you wish it to be.
